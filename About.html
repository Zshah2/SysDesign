Our Campus

Located on 604 beautiful acres, Boolean University's campus combines historic architecture with state-of-the-art facilities. From our modern science labs to our vibrant student centers, every space is designed to foster learning, collaboration, and community.

Our History

Founded in 1968, Boolean University has a rich tradition of academic excellence and social progress. From our early days as a pioneering institution to our current status as a leading liberal arts college, we have remained committed to accessibility, diversity, and transformative education.
